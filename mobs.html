Basic Hostile Mobs

In this section, we weill learn about the common hostile mobs in minecraft that you'll surely often encounter if you're still not prepared.

Zombie

Zombies are slow-moving, undead creatures that are common in Minecraft. Zombies are aggressive and will attack players on sight.

Learn More
Skeleton

Skeletons are hostile mobs that are armed with bows and arrows with the exception of wither skeleton (wielded with stone sword) and will attack players from a distance.

Learn More
Creeper

Creepers are hostile mobs that will silently approach players and explode upon close proximity. Shield is also an effective way to block the explosion

Learn More
Spiders and mites

Spiders and mites are hostile mobs that can be found in different biomes. Spiders can climb walls so building a tower block to escape is useless.

Learn More
Enderman

Endermen are hostile mobs that are found in the End dimension. Endermen are known for their teleportation abilities and will attack players if they are looked at directly.

Learn More
Phantom

Phantoms are hostile flying mobs that spawn at night in the Overworld. They are most active during full moons. Phantoms will attack players if they haven't slept in a while.

Learn More
Zombie Villager

zombie villagers are mobs that look similar to normal villagers but have zombie-colored flesh and attack players. They can be created when a normal zombie kills a villager, or they can spawn naturally.

Learn More
Slime

Slimes are bouncy, cube-shaped hostile mobs that spawn deep underground in particular chunks, or in swamp biomes. They attack by jumping at their targets, come in three sizes and larger slimes can split into smaller ones on death.
